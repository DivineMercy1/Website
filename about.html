What kind of work interests you?

I like doing any kind of work that allows me to learn something. From front end to back end development, it's all fun to me. I've done database development, gameplay programming, AI programming, web development, user interface programming, and more - it never gets dull and I am always learning from it; therefore, any kind of work that tests my skills and patience interests me.

What was your upbringing like?

Since I was 3 years old I played video games. I remember getting a transparent purple GameBoy Color when it came out, playing Super Mario, Pokemon, and other games on it. After a while I started thinking about what went into a video game: how it was made, how it was designed, the audio work that was involved, the story-telling components, the gameplay that made it unique, etc. etc. These thoughts always confused me and I never understood it all until I decided to teach myself programming and learn Unity 3D in high school where I took on the challenge of creating a video game for my Capstone Project. Making a game is beyond difficult, and doing it alone is a never ending mission essentially. Video games were my passion, still are, and is what keeps me intrigued as I see so many advancements in the industry every year.
